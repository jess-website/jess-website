Jessy's Perfect Fudge Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes + Chill Time.

Servings: About 2lbs / 1kg

If you're looking for the perfect and easiest dessert to make - look no further than this crowdpleasing fudge recipe.

ALLEGEN WARNING: THIS RECIPE CONTAINS NUTS.

If you've never cooked or baked before, don't worry. All you really need is a single pot, a spoon or spatula and a stove - or even a microwave.

The Best Ways To Melt Chocolate

Ingredients

  • 3 cups (750 mL) semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 can (300 mL) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tsp (10 mL) vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (125 mL) chopped nuts

If that long list of ingredients didn't scare you Hopefully it didn't...I know there is a lot of chocolate...nevermind , let's get into the process of making this delicious fudge.

Instructions

  1. Melt chocolate chips with condensed milk in a sauce pan, on low heat.
  2. Remove the pan from heat; stir in vanilla and nuts.
  3. Spread evenly in parchment paper-lined 8-inch (20cm) square pan
  4. Chill 4 hours or until firm. Personally, I prefer to let it chill overnight. But hey, whatever works for you.
  5. Remove from pan and peel off paper. Cut into squares.
  6. Enjoy! Or else...
